About us

“Smart Plaza“ business center by Malkhasyanshin is the new business center designed and being built by “MalkhasyanShin”, a company having a years of experience and enjoying customer trust. It is a place where business environment is combined with recreation areas. Afour-story premium class building – on the roof is located a heated outdoor swimming pool that will be open all the year around. In winter the glass cover will allow you to enjoy the cold winter days in a warm environment. On the fourth floor you can find a gym equipped with the best gym machines produced by leading companies, and of course, high-quality trainers having international qualifications. The first two floors are designed as service halls for Armenian and international brands where you can find pocket-friendly services and products pleasant to any taste. From entertainment and recreation areas you can make a smooth move and appear on the third floor of the business complex - a business environment designed for productive and efficient work. Here are bright and large office areas excellent for the promotion of your business ideas. “Smart Plaza” business center by Malkhasyanshin – a business center with two separate entrances where every detail is taken into consideration for your productive working day and proper rest.
